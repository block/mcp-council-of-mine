## Usage in Debates

When a topic is presented to the council:

1. **Opinion Formation** - Each member independently evaluates the topic through their unique lens
2. **Voting Phase** - Members vote for opinions that align with their values (excluding their own)
3. **Synthesis** - An AI-generated conclusion incorporates all perspectives, highlighting winning viewpoints while acknowledging other insights
4. **Result** - A balanced, multi-faceted conclusion that benefits from diverse expertise

This process mirrors real-world decision-making where the best outcomes emerge from respectful debate among experts with different specialties and worldviews.
